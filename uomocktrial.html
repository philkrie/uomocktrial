CHAMPIONSHIP ASPIRATION

UOMT is a team on the rise, and qualified to our first ever ORCS last year! This year we expect to build on our accomplishments and qualify to the National Championship Tournament.


TRAVEL

Working with Oregon Forensics, UOMT is well funded and travels to several tournaments every year! Whether chugging along in a 15 person van or flying high in a 737, UOMT travels in style!


LEARN THE LAW

Whether you perform as a witness or a lawyer, UOMT will give to a glimpse into the courtroom procedure and how a real trial works, absolute musts for aspiring lawyers!

News

UOMT recently visited Boise, Idaho, location of the Pacific Northwest AMTA Regional. Despite both teams failing to advance, the team had a great time and learned a lot of lessons to carry into next year. Oregon A competed against Oregon State, Cal, Gonzaga, and Washington, while Oregon B comepeted against Reed, Washington, Cal, and Gonzaga. Attorney Riley Olson won an All-Region Attorney award with 18 ranks!
