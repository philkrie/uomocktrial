WELCOME

Join us in our quest for a National Championship; visit collegemocktrial.org for more information about collegiate Mock Trial.

CHAMPIONSHIP ASPIRATION

UOMT is a team on the rise and qualified to our first ever National Championship in 2016! We tied for 6th in our division and placed 19th overall out of 48 teams. This year we expect to build on our accomplishments.


TRAVEL

Working with Oregon Forensics, UOMT is well funded and travels to several tournaments every year! Whether chugging along in a 15 person van or flying high in a 737, UOMT travels in style!


LEARN THE LAW

Whether you perform as a witness or a lawyer, UOMT will give to a glimpse into the courtroom procedure and how a real trial works, absolute musts for aspiring lawyers!

IMMORTALITY

Oregon 1389 completes the single greatest season in team history, finishing 10th place in their division and 19th overall.

WE ARE ALL WITNESSES

Phillip Kriegel becomes the first All-American in Oregon history, earning All-American honors as a witness on both the prosecution and the defense. He was the only witness at the tournament to earn the award on both sides.

HONORABLE SHOWING

Oregon 1390 finished 4-3-1 at the Boise Regional and earns a spot on the open bid list!

UPSET SPECIAL

Oregon 1647 finished 4-4 at one of the most difficult Regionals in the country in sunny Fresno. On their way to their impressive results, they took ballots from UCLA B, and UC Irvine A!
