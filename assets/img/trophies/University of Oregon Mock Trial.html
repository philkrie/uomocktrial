Greg Mina

Greg is a senior from Colorado Springs, CO, pursuing a double major in Political Science and Spanish while studying within the Clark Honors College. Greg is entering his fourth year with UOMT, and third as President. In high school, Greg earned two outstanding attorney awards while leading his team to two state championship rounds, and garnered the most individual ranks in the state in 2011. Competing for UOMT, Greg served as captain of the first ORCS team in school history, and was the first UOMT competitor to earn all 20 possible individual ranks at a tournament.
