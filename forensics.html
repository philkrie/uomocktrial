Forensics

United we stand, divided we fall

UOMT is lucky to find itself within the Forensics Department as a whole. UOMT is one of many competitive branches of the Forensics team here at UO, and our collaboration with the Forensics department and staff ensures the necessary support for UOMT competitors. While competing for UOMT, competitors are given the option to take a class designed for Forensics competitors specifically, HC399H, taught by expert speech educator and the Director of Forensics here at UO, Dr. Trond Jacobsen. UOMT has experienced unprecedented success under the Forensics umbrella and coaching of Associate Director of Forensics Steve Clemmons, and looks forward to many more successful years in collaboration with the Forensics Department.
