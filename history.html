History

A long, long time ago, in a court room far, far away...
The University of Oregon Mock Trial Team was founded in 2010.

Recognized by the Associated Students of the University of Oregon the next year, it fielded two teams at the University of Portland Regionals Tournament in 2012 and two teams in 2013.

In January of 2014, the team attended its first ever invitational tournament, the Third Annual Lee Allen Memorial Invitational in Berkeley, California. In February of the same year, Oregon Mock Trial qualified a team to ORCS for the first time in program history, attending the Newport Beach ORC in March.
